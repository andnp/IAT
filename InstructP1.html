Introversion/Extraversion IAT Demo Instructions

You will be shown words one at a time. Your task is to sort these words into two categories. To sort words place your left index finger on the 'f' key of your keyboard and your right index finger on the 'j' key of your keyboard.

For this first exercise you will be shown words that can be classified as traits common to Introverts and traits common to Extraverts. Please sort commonly introverted traits to the left by pressing the 'f' key, and commonly extraverted traits to the right by pressing the 'j' key.
